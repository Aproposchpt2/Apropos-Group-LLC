Overview

Apropos Group LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates aproposgroupllc.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website or submit an inquiry, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you submit directly through our contact form:

  • Name / Contracting Officer — to identify who is making the inquiry
  • Agency or Organization name — to understand the source of the inquiry
  • Email address — to respond to your inquiry
  • Phone number — if provided voluntarily
  • Opportunity or Solicitation number — if applicable
  • Requirement description — the content of your inquiry

We do not collect financial information, payment details, or sensitive personal data through this website.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your capability briefing request or government inquiry
  • To send you requested documentation (capability statements, past performance records, etc.)
  • To maintain records required for federal contracting purposes
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your inquiry data is used only to respond to your request.

Data Retention

Inquiry records are retained for a period consistent with federal contracting record-keeping requirements, typically 3 years. You may request deletion of your record by contacting us directly.

Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of information we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal information
